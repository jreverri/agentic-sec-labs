# Lab 0: Onboarding

**Overview:**
This guide verifies you have a Google Gemini account to use as your **"Agentic Brain"** for the upcoming Cybersecurity Labs. If you already have access to a chatbot, feel free to proceed to Lab 1 and use your preferred chatbot instead.

**Prerequisites:** A Google Account (Gmail).

---
## What is an Agent? (Brief Intro)

Unlike a standard chatbot, an **Agent** is designed to _do_ things. It has:
1. **A Persona:** A role (e.g., Security Operator).
2. **Tools:** Functions it can call (e.g., check_ip_reputation).
3. **Constraints:** Rules it must follow (e.g., "Always check logs first").

In these labs, **YOU** will act as the Simulator, feeding the Agent the results of its tool calls allowing you to peer into the processes of the Agent and demystify how and why it is operating. In order to facilitate this, we will use a chatbot with lab provided **System Instructions**.

---
## Option A: Google Gemini (Recommended)
*Easiest for quick access.*

1.  **Navigate to:** [https://gemini.google.com/](https://gemini.google.com/)
2.  **Sign In:** Log in with your Google Account.
3.  **Ready:** You can now start chatting. Note that you will paste the "System Instructions" directly into the chat window instead of a dedicated box.

## Option B: Google AI Studio (Advanced)
*Best for a "developer" experience with dedicated system instructions.*

1.  **Navigate to:** [https://aistudio.google.com/](https://aistudio.google.com/)
2.  **Sign In:** Click **"Sign in to Google AI Studio"** and use your Google credentials.
3.  **Accept Terms:** If prompted, agree to the Terms of Service.
4.  **Create Prompt:**
    *   Click the **"Create New"** button (top left).
    *   Select **"Chat Prompt"**.
5.  **Ready:** You will see a "System Instructions" box on the top and a "Type something" box at the bottom. You are ready for Lab 1.
